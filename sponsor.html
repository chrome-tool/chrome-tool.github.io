Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about sponsoring? Find answers below.

How is my sponsorship used?

Your sponsorship directly supports development time, server costs, and ongoing maintenance of the extension. It helps keep the extension free for everyone while ensuring it continues to improve.

Can I cancel my monthly sponsorship?

Yes, you can cancel your monthly sponsorship at any time. Your support for any duration is greatly appreciated.

Are there any exclusive features for sponsors?

Yes, depending on your sponsorship tier, you may get early access to new features, voting rights on feature prioritization, and direct support channels.
