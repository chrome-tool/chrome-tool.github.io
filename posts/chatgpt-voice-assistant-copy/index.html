About the Extension

Description:#

ChatGPT Voice Assistant is an intelligent Chrome extension designed to bring you a more convenient online communication experience. Whether you’re browsing the web or need to quickly send a message, simply click the “Voice” button to interact with ChatGPT via voice input. ChatGPT will convert your voice messages into text and read back the results in the language of your choice. This extension supports multiple language options and adjustable reading speeds, ensuring smooth and efficient communication.

Key Features:#

  • Interact with ChatGPT through voice input without the need for keyboard typing.
  • Support for multiple language options to meet the needs of - users worldwide.
  • Read back results to provide diverse communication methods.
  • Adjustable reading speed to suit individual preferences.
  • User-friendly interface design for easy enjoyment of intelligent communication.
